---
title: "Audience limit for App + Web properties increased from 50 to 100 "
date: 2020-06-09T13:16:14.249Z
template: post
---
Google Analytics recently announced that for App + Web Properties they have increased the number of audiences you can create from 50 to 100.

Audiences in Analytics are users that you group together based on any combination of attributes that is meaningful to your business. Predefined ones include things like ( New Users, Returning Users, Users who completed a goal conversion, etc ).

Previously in Google analytics, you can have a maximum of 50 audiences published to an Analytics account, and a maximum of 20 published to any single property within that account.
